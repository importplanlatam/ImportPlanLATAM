Frequently Asked Questions

What does the subscription include?

The subscription provides access to updated guides, checklists, cost calculators, and monthly regulatory alerts. Premium tiers include one-to-one advisory and document processing support for an additional fee.

Which countries are covered?

ImportPlan LATAM currently covers imports into Argentina from Brazil, Chile, Uruguay, and Paraguay. We plan to expand coverage as the service matures.

How often are regulatory updates published?

We monitor changes continuously and send monthly digest alerts. Critical changes that may affect operations are sent as immediate notifications.

Can you help file documents?

Yes — document filling and filing is available as an optional paid service. Contact us through the demo form to discuss your specific needs and pricing.

How can I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions can be canceled at any time from your account dashboard or by contacting support. Cancellation details and refund policies vary by plan tier.
